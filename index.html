About iSchoola

Founded in 2007 under the name Cerebrom Business Solutions, iSchoola launched its first program in 2009 as a school and educational institution management system. Today, iSchoola expands across investment, operations, and financing of schools—driven by a commitment to educational quality and future readiness.

Vision

Schools as launchpads for lifelong success—leaders, thinkers, innovators.

Model

Student-centered ecosystem: rigorous academics, projects, leadership, and family engagement.

Quality

Research-based, adaptive, and aligned to modern educational trends—grounded in community needs.
